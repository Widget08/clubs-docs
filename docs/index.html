Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my first round of golf? +
To start your first round, tap the "New Round" button on the home screen or rounds tab. Search for your golf course or create a new one if it doesn't exist. Add any weather conditions or notes, then tap "Start Round" to begin tracking your game.
Can I edit my scores after completing a hole? +
Yes! You can edit hole scores even after completing them. Simply tap the edit button on any completed hole card to modify your score or swing details.
How is my handicap calculated? +
Your handicap can be manually entered in your profile settings. The app tracks your scores to help you monitor your improvement over time, but official handicap calculations should follow your local golf association's guidelines.
What's the difference between Quick Swing and Detailed Swing? +
Quick Swing adds a basic stroke to your count instantly. Detailed Swing allows you to specify the type of swing (drive, putt, chip, etc.), club used, and whether it was successful. Use Quick Swing for faster play and Detailed Swing for comprehensive tracking.
Can I create and manage golf courses? +
Yes! You can create new golf courses if they don't exist in our database. As the course creator, you become the course administrator and can edit course details, add hole information including par and distance, and manage course settings.
Is my golf data private and secure? +
Yes, your personal golf data is private and secure. Only you can see your rounds, scores, and statistics. Course information is shared publicly to help other golfers find and play at different courses.
